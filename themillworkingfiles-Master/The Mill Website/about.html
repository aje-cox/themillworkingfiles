Our Vision

About Us

In early 2017, James Harris, Managing Director of The Mill W&C, realised his dream 5 years in the making for a health-foods store accessible and inviting to everyone, being able to cater to every person’s individual needs within the journey for a better way of living in a new, exciting business. The Mill W&C was formed as a result of James’ 12 years experience as an entrepreneur and marketing and sales manager within the hospitality and retail industry. The business model our store is based upon stems from our concerns for the large amount of waste and impact the modern-day way of living has on our world, with the packaging of health foods and other everyday goods we have found is either in extremely small quantities and high prices or in bulk, resulting in the waste of produce and money. But now, through our vision we seek to change this, catering to every person’s needs and way of living, saving you money. We strive at providing you with excellent service and products through our dedicated team here at The Mill, including Operations Manager Charlotte Ryan and Marketing Manager Alexander Rooke. We look forward to helping you on your journey for a healthier way of living.
